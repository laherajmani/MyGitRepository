Frequently asked questions FAQs

WHY SHOULD I BUY GROCERIES ON ORGANIKONG?

OrganiKong offers the best of grocery shopping experience where the customer can get a wide range of products offered by local kirana stores who will deliver at their home. Just use our user friendly website and make orders.

HOW DO I REGISTER?

You need to visit our website www.OrganiKong.com and select “New User Log In” in the Home page. Fill in the required details and select create “Create Account”. New users can also can also create new account while shopping, through his/her Gmail or Facebook accounts.

ARE THERE ANY PRE-REQUISITES FOR ORDERING ON ORGANIKONG?

You need have a valid residential address in Delhi/ NCR Region so that the order could be delivered there. To make an online order at our website, an e-mail id and a mobile number is required.

WHAT IS MY ACCOUNT?

My Account is the section you reach after you log in at www.organikong.com. My Account allows you to track your active orders, credit note details as well as see your order history and update your contact details.

HOW WILL THE DELIVERY BE DONE?

As per the customer location, the order will be home delivered by the nearest grocery vendor (partner with OrganiKong) who is offering the product at the minimum price.

WHAT KIND OF PRODUCTS DO YOU SELL?

OrganiKong sell all the categories of groceries required at home. These include grocery & staples, confectionary, dairy, bakery, packed foods, personal care, households etc.

WHAT ARE THE MODES OF PAYMENT?

You can pay for your order on organikong.com using the following modes of payment: a. Cash on delivery (pay on order arrival) b. Online payment (Credit and debit cards).

IF I PAY BY CREDIT CARD HOW DO I GET THE AMOUNT BACK FOR ITEMS NOT DELIVERED?

If we are not able to delivery all the products in your order and you have already paid for them online, the balance amount will be credited to your OrganiKong account. The customer can contact our customer support team and we will refund it back on to your card within 14 days.

WHAT IS THE MINIMUM ORDER VALUE FOR DELIVERY?

The minimum order value of the product for delivery is Rs. 500. Orders less than this will not be processed.

WHAT WILL BE THE DELIVEY PERIOD FOR THE PRODUCT ORDERED?

The product being ordered online will be delivered within 24 hours after the order is placed.

IS SAME DAY DELIVERY APPLICABLE TO ONLY A FEW PRODUCTS OR ALL PRODUCTS?

Delivery is applicable to all products displayed in the OrganiKong website (which is added to the cart).

HOW DO I RESET MY PASSWORD?

You need to enter your email address on the Login page and click on forgot password. An email with a reset password will be sent to your email address. With this, you can change your password.

CAN I HAVE MULTIPLE REGISTRATIONS?

Each email address and contact phone number can only be associated with one www.OrganiKong.com account.

WHAT IS THE MEANING OF PAY ON DELIVERY?

It is the most convenient way of making payments. The customer can pay the price of the product being delivered when it reaches his/her hand. This helps when a customer does not have any online payments mode.

WHAT ARE MULTIPLE ADRESSES?

Customers can enter multiple delivery addresses at the time of registration. While shopping the customer need to select one deliver address where the order can be delivered.

HOW DO I CONTACT CUSTOMER SERVICE?

Click to Contact Us

WHAT ARE YOUR TIMINGS TO CONTACT CUSTOMER SERVICE?

You can call us from 9AM to 6 PM.

WHAT AREAS DO YOU DELIVER TO? ARE THE RATES THE SAME?

We have different kirana stores across Delhi as our partners who home deliver goods within their delivery radius. The products will be sold at or below the MRP.

IS IT SAFE TO SHOP USING ONLINE PAYMENT METHODS ON ORGANIKONG?

All the transactions for the online grocery shopping will be done through secure payment gateway. Your privacy will be taken care of and all the personal data will be kept confidential.

WHEN AND HOW CAN I CANCEL AN ORDER?

A customer can cancel his/her order anytime up to 2 hours after the online order placement. You can also call us at our customer care to cancel the orders but within the operational hours of our office. In case of online payment the amount will be refunded.
